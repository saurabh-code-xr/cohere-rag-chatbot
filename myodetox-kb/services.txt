## Physiotherapy

Our physiotherapy treatments address pain, injuries, and movement issues through manual therapy, exercise, and education. Sessions may include stretching, mobilizations, and strengthening routines.

## Chiropractic

Chiropractic care focuses on joint health, spinal alignment, and nervous system function. It can support recovery from back pain, tension, and mobility issues.

## Registered Massage Therapy (RMT)

RMT involves therapeutic massage techniques to reduce muscle tension, improve circulation, and relieve pain or stress-related conditions.
